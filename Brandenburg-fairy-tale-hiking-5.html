Crossroad Confusion

Oh no! A crossroads. Where are we supposed to go? Princess Hammera has conjured up a maze of many paths to throw the evil wizard Hansemichel off her trail.

However, with the help of her friends the trees, she has left you a signpost that only you can see.

Unfortunately, grown-ups are still in the dark. Show the adults the way and find the enchanted arrow on the forest floor.
