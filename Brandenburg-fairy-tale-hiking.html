Hammera & the Evil Hansemichel

An interactive journey in which your little ones will feel like heroes! Only with their help can the princess be saved. Look forward to a varied hike that will fly by.

Parents, children, grandma and grandpa will talk about this lovingly told adventure with their mouths open for a long time to come.

Our FairyTrail begins with a short walk. Take the path to the left around the Oberförsterei until you reach the sheltered picnic bench.

Once there, press the "Start" button and the app will begin. Have fun!

Mobile phone connectivity may not be ideal in the forest. Maybe download the eBook provided below if you have problems with your connection.
